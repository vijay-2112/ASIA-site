Welcome To Cal-Asia Pte Ltd

Cal-Asia Pte Ltd provides quality and professional work in multivendor calibration & Repair services. Head quarter in Singapore and emerging your global one stop service provider.

Our RF team has a combined of over 25 years of experience in this Industry. With our care and expertise, allows you to be at ease in the maintenance and monitoring of your equipment. Our calibration is traceable to NIST & ISO17025.

We have acquired VK Calibration services in 2021 to expand our offering to our customer in terms of mechanical calibration covering temperature , torque , dimension , pressure & flow.
